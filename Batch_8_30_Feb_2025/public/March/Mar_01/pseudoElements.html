News Item 1

Rachel Accurso, better known as Ms. Rachel, is bringing her beloved educational content to Netflix. With a curated four-episode series debuting on January 27, the show will feature interactive lessons on speech, letters, numbers, and nursery rhymes, with subtitles in 33 languages. Aimed at toddlers, this move is part of Netflix’s strategy to attract younger audiences while competing with YouTube. With more episodes planned for 2025, Ms. Rachel’s transition signals a growing synergy between digital creators and traditional streaming platforms.
